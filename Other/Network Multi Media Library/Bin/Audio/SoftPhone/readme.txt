Running:

1. Right click on the phone panel.
2. In the popup choose "Audio settings".
3. Press "Choose codec" and choose either PCM or better Speex if you have it. 
(if you do not have Speex please install it form http://www.republika.pl/roed/speexw/download/speexw.exe)
4. Choose the sound device (if you have several).
6. Set wave sound params (BPS, Frequency, Stereo/Mono).
5. Press "Ok".
6. Right click on the phone panel again.
7. Press "Choose codec" and choose either PCM or better Speex if you have it. 
(if you do not have Speex please install it form http://www.republika.pl/roed/speexw/download/speexw.exe)
Note, for Speex BPS should be 16 bits.
It's better to use Speex modes from 8000 bps or higher. 
8. In the popup choose "Phone settings".
9. Choose TCP port.
10.Press Ok.

Repeat 1-10 for another SoftPhone application on the other side of the network.

11.Press "Add contact" on the phone screen.
12.Input alias and host:port of the another SoftPhone application.
13.Press call button.
14.You should hear waiting tone on your side and ringing tone on the other.
15.Other party should press call button on their site.
16.Enjoy.
