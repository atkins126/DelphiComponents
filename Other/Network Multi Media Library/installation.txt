
1. Extract the NMM lib somewhere on your PC.
2. In the Delphi7 open NMM70.dpk, press "Compile" and then "Install".
3. Install NMMRC70.dpk and NMMAudio70.dpk the same way.
4. Add NMPath\Source\ and NMPath\Source\Frames\ into your Delphi library path
5. Try compiling the demos.

If you cannot compile tyhe demos, you may download them compiled from 
www.i-software.narod.ru.
