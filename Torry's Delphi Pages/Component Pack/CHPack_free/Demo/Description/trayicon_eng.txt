- Component, to display an Icon in that task bar (beside the clock)
- over an Iconlist can different Icons be loaded
- Icon can "flash" in that task bar
- In the text mode a formatted text can be displayed in place of the Icons
- the visibility of the icons and the task bar entry can be changed in 4 levels.
  You can select "always show", "never show", "only in the minimized status show" 
  and "only in the maximized status show"
